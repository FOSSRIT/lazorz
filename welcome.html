What is Lazorz?

Lazorz is an Open Source educational game about the physics of light.
In it's current incarnation it helps demonstrate the concepts of reflection
and color filtering. The development team has plans to include other
concepts such as refraction and prisms in future releases.

How do you win?

The game objective is to reflect the beam
by using rotating mirrors
from the starting tile
to the end tile
occasionally projected through a filter.
If you make a mistake, you can always delete tiles
